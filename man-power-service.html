About Us

Is one of the leading Manpower Agency in Private Sector and Government offering global services to well reputed companies for their manpower requirements. The leadership of Mr. Vivek Dutta, an academic lawyer, post gaining lawful experience, working as a criminal lawyer and specializing in Labor Laws and member of regional provident fund committee has taken the organization into a high success rate that too in short span of time.

Well skilled and unskilled manpower adhering to spirit of innovating developed products, utilize their industrial expertise in delivering equipment with reduced maintenance cost.
