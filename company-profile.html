Company Profile

Home / Company Profile

In 2007, the cornerstone of an innovative value-driven company - Brilltech Engineers Pvt. Ltd., was laid under the supervision of the honorable chairman Mr. Nitin Pratap Singh. With customers centric approach, the company caters to its clients with a huge assortment of products that includes Electrical Panel , Package Substation, Electrical Rising Mains, Diesel Generator, Electrical Cable, Earthing Strips, Electrical Transformer, Bus Duct, Meter Board Panel, Cable Tray, Chemical Earthing System, Lightning Arrestor, etc. The deep and in-depth knowledge of the CEO in the respective domain has helped the company to climb the soaring heights of success.

Infrastructure

State-of-the-art infrastructure is backed with modern manufacturing machines that help in producing Cable Tray that comply with the international paradigms. The machines are also upgraded with time to ensure the best of the quality goods are delivered to the customers.

Quality Assurance

With the motive to deliver quality Bus Duct and services, various quality checks areperformed right from the procurement of the raw materials, to the manufacturing unit to the final packaging of the goods. Various stringent quality tests are performed to make sure of the international quality standard.

Team

Along with modern technology, a diversified workforce is employed to take care of the manufacturing of electrical goods and provide professional turnkey solutions. The team includes supervisors, engineers, technicians, designers, & many others.

Vision
